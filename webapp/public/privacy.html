Privacy Policy for Penny4Thots

Last Updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Penny4Thots ("we", "our", or "us") operates a decentralized application ("App") that enables users to participate in prediction-based markets powered by blockchain technology.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App via web or mobile platforms, including any Android application distributed through the Google Play Store.

By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Wallet Information

When you use our App, you may connect a blockchain wallet. We may collect and process:

  • Public wallet address
  • Transaction history related to our smart contracts

Note: Wallet addresses may be considered personal data under certain jurisdictions.

2.2 Blockchain Data

All blockchain transactions are:

  • Public
  • Immutable
  • Stored permanently on decentralized networks

We do not control or store this data, but we may access and display it through the App.

2.3 Device and Technical Data

When accessing the App via mobile or web, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • Browser type
  • App usage data (e.g. interactions, performance logs)

2.4 Analytics Data

We may use third-party analytics tools to understand usage patterns and improve performance. These tools may collect:

  • Session duration
  • Pages/screens visited
  • Interaction events

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Facilitate blockchain transactions
  • Display market data and user interactions
  • Improve App functionality and performance
  • Detect, prevent, and mitigate abuse or fraudulent activity
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

We may rely on third-party providers, including but not limited to:

  • Blockchain infrastructure providers (e.g. RPC nodes)
  • Wallet connection services (e.g. WalletConnect or embedded wallets)
  • Analytics providers

These third parties may process limited data as necessary to provide their services.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited data:

  • With service providers to operate the App
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect rights, safety, and integrity of the platform

6. Data Retention

  • Off-chain data is retained only as long as necessary for operational purposes.
  • On-chain data is permanent and cannot be deleted.
